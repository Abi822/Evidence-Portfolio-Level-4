Web Theory and Business Website Assignment


  • Part A, B and C – Theory questions
  • Part D – Website
  • Part A: Copyright Regulations & Issues in New Zealand
  • Part B: Critique screen design
  • Part C: Internet Basics
  • Part D: Creating a basic web site

Part A, B, and C are to be done using a Google Form which the tutor will open at an appropriate time. They will be a mixture of short answers, and Multi-Choice. On the short answer section there must be at least a paragraph on each point – no one word answers will be accepted. This is an individual assignment. The aim is to produce an assignment that shows the level of your understanding of copyright, and plagiarism, critique of screen design fundamentals in relationship to web site design and show your knowledge of Internet basics.

You must have completed your Business Plan for your fictitious computer repair business. You will make a website based on this business. There needs to be at least five pages using HTML5 and CSS. (See part D on page 3) By completing the website, you will show your basic understanding of HTML5 and CSS coding. This assignment is to be submitted as a zip file in Moodle on or before the due date and time. Format for submission: IDFirst_Last.zip Example: (123456789Fred_Frog.zip) Any file with a different compression format will not be accepted; (e.g .rar, .7zip etc) Lecturer: John Achilles – john.achilles@toiohomai.ac.nz Full marking rubrics begin on page 5.
